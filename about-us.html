Oriculo's Resort: A Legacy of Warmth ✨

Here in Bulalacao, every guest is truly family—a philosophy rooted deep in our history, stretching back to our beloved patriarch, Lolo Oriculo.
It was Lolo Oriculo who acquired this beautiful land with his pension savings, dreaming of a lasting pamana (legacy) for his children.
That spirit of open-hearted hospitality is the very essence of Oriculo's Resort. We want you to feel as though you're coming home by the sea.
